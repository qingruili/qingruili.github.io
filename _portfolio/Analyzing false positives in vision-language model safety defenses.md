Conducted a research study on AdaShield, a safety defense for vision-language models, evaluating its effectiveness against attacks and its tendency to over-block benign inputs. Designed experiments using CLIP-based similarity and real-world health/finance data, revealing a 25–44% false positive rate under natural user queries while maintaining 90–100% attack blocking performance.
