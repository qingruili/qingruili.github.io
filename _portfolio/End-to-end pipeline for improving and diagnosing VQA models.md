Developed an end-to-end pipeline for Vision-Language Models (VQA), combining model optimization and diagnostic evaluation:

• Step 1 (Model Tuning): Applied parameter-efficient fine-tuning (LoRA, Adapters, IA3) on BLIP-2, achieving +7% accuracy improvement while maintaining low training cost.

• Step 2 (Model Evaluation): Designed a question-type-aware evaluation framework (GQA taxonomy, 132K samples), uncovering up to 60% performance variation and identifying weaknesses in relational reasoning and spatial understanding.
